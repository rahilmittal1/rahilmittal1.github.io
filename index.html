finding nemo rahil

i’ve been struggling with the concept of "self" for a while. i barely remember me anymore. sometimes i miss me. but that’s not a bad thing.

because for my whole life, i derived my sense of identity strictly through my output. eventually i burnt myself out from the things i did that yielded this output. i tried everything to remember who i was. i couldn’t.

i even went 10 days without speaking a single word. total silence without a phone or internet in the middle of a forest in illinois - 240 hours of undisturbed meditation in hopes of figuring out the self (vipassana).
that kinda helped tbh.

i now find myself in a rabbit hole of… whatever you want to call it - spirituality? metaphysics? autism?

past lives

i spent my entire teenage life trading crypto. at age 14, i made an amount of money that no 14 year old should get near. and then i lost most of it all. i’m not proud of it, but it was the most expensive education i ever received. i owe a lot of who i am to this teenage pursuit.

i was a drummer and listened to a lot of music. i played the drums for 12 years and performed in front of thousands and taught a bunch of people along the way. fell out of love with the instrument one day in 2024. in another life, i shall dust the dust off my drumsticks. until then, i must tend to the other relics in this museum.
